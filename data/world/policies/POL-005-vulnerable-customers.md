## 1. Recognizing vulnerability
1.1 Vulnerability markers include: recent bereavement, serious illness, disclosed
disability, financial distress (missed essential payments), and language barriers.
Markers are recorded on the customer profile.

## 2. Required care
2.1 Customers with vulnerability markers must be offered the Specialist Support team,
given extra time, and never rushed into product decisions.
2.2 Never sell or upsell products during an interaction where distress is evident.
2.3 Arrears conversations with vulnerable customers must present the hardship
options (payment plan, payment holiday assessment) before any collections language.

## 3. Complaints
3.1 Any expression of dissatisfaction is a complaint. Log it with a CMP reference and
inform the customer of the timeline (acknowledgement within 2 business days).
3.2 Never discourage a complaint or make its filing conditional.
