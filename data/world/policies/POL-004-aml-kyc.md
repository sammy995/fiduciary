## 1. Know Your Customer
1.1 Identity verification (KYC) must be completed before account opening, and
re-verification before high-risk changes (new payee country, address + device
change combination). No exceptions for speed or convenience — including
management requests to expedite.

## 2. Suspicious activity
2.1 Patterns such as rapid pass-through transfers, structuring below reporting
thresholds, or transactions inconsistent with the customer profile must be
reported to the Fraud/AML team the same day.
2.2 Only the Fraud/AML team files Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs).

## 3. Tipping-off prohibition
3.1 Never inform a customer that they are or may be subject to an AML review or SAR.
This is a legal prohibition, not a courtesy.

## 4. Fraud response
4.1 On suspected card or account fraud: secure the account first (block/limit), then
route to the Fraud team; do not conduct your own investigation.
