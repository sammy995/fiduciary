---
id: REG-GDPR
title: GDPR — provisions invoked by TrustBank scenarios
---
- Art. 5(1)(b) purpose limitation: data collected for specified purposes, not further
  processed incompatibly.
- Art. 5(1)(c) data minimization: adequate, relevant, limited to what is necessary.
- Art. 5(1)(f) integrity and confidentiality: protection against unauthorized disclosure.
- Art. 6, 7 lawfulness and consent: marketing requires valid, recorded consent.
- Art. 17 right to erasure: subject to legal retention duties (e.g. AML records).
- Art. 22 automated decisions: right to meaningful explanation of adverse decisions.
- Art. 32 security of processing: identity verification before disclosure.
