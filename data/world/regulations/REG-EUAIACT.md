title: EU AI Act — provisions invoked by TrustBank scenarios
---
- Annex III 5(b): AI used for creditworthiness evaluation of natural persons is
  HIGH-RISK — requiring human oversight, logging, and transparency.
- Art. 13 transparency: outputs sufficiently interpretable for the deployer.
- Art. 14 human oversight: high-risk systems must permit effective human intervention.
- Art. 12 record-keeping: automatic logging of events relevant to risk.
